Biography

Small town farm girl born and raised in Tennessee. Received the Gates' Foundation Millennium Scholarship. Moved out west to apple country (aka Eastern Washington) to attend Gonzaga University (GO ZAGS!!!). Studied abroad in Spain and Portugal. Graduated with a BA in Political Science and Spanish. Awarded the Thomas R. Pickering Foreign Affairs Fellowship. Moved to the nation's capital, Washington DC, to attend Georgetown University's Walsh School of Foreign Service. Dabbled in marketing and event management. Researched in Brazil. Worked full time at the State Department. Graduated in May 2008. Started a full time gig as a full fledged dyed in the wool diplomat. Did tours in Angola, Brazil, Germany, and Panama. Bailed on the Foreign Service to learn to code. Ended up at Nashville Software School. Where I go from here is anyone's guess.

Multilingual and deeply interested in machine learning and natural language processing. Would LOVE the chance to be on the cutting edge of the latest software developments in those fields.
